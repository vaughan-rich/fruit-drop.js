Fruit-Drop.js does not support touch controls! View full-screen on a desktop/laptop to see what you're missing.

How to Play: Catch the fruit in the basket - miss one and you lose a life! Grab a golden fruit for a pick-me-up, but touch a rotten one and you'll ruin the batch! Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move.
